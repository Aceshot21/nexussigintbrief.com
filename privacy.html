01 Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide Directly

When you subscribe to NEXUS Signal Brief or create an account, we collect information you provide, which may include:

  • Your name and email address (required to deliver the newsletter)
  • Your subscription tier selection (Free, Premium, or ULTRA)
  • Communications you send to us, including support requests, accessibility inquiries, and any other correspondence

We do not solicit or collect personal financial information such as your investment objectives, risk tolerance, net worth, account balances, or investment experience. Consistent with our Disclaimers and Terms of Use, NEXUS Signal Brief does not customize its recommendations based on any individual subscriber's financial circumstances.

B. Payment Information

Paid subscriptions are processed through our third-party newsletter and subscription platform, Beehiiv. Payment card information (such as credit card numbers) is collected and processed directly by Beehiiv's payment processor. NEXUS Signal Brief does not receive, store, or process your full payment card details. We may receive limited transaction information such as the last four digits of your card, billing country, and subscription status for account management purposes.

C. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the Site or open our newsletter emails, certain information is collected automatically, which may include:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (country or region)
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
  • Newsletter open rates, click-through activity, and email client type
  • Device identifiers and screen resolution

This information is collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies described further in Section 5 below, as well as through analytics provided by our service providers.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the NEXUS Signal Brief newsletter and any other subscription content you have enrolled in
  • To process and manage your subscription, including billing, renewals, and cancellations
  • To communicate with you about your account, subscription status, and any changes to our services, policies, or terms
  • To respond to your inquiries, support requests, and accessibility needs
  • To monitor and improve the quality, reliability, and performance of the Site and newsletter
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud, security incidents, and violations of our Terms of Use
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from government or regulatory authorities, and enforce our rights
  • To analyze aggregate, non-identifying usage trends for internal reporting and service improvement

We do not use your personal information to build individual financial profiles or to tailor investment recommendations to your specific circumstances. All newsletter content is impersonal in nature and published uniformly to all subscribers within the same subscription tier.

  • With Your Consent. We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

05 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to support basic functionality and to understand how visitors use the Site. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device by a website you visit.

Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies. Required for the Site to function, including maintaining session state and remembering your subscription login. These cannot be disabled without affecting Site functionality.
  • Functional Cookies. Used to remember your preferences and improve your experience on the Site.
  • Analytics Cookies. Used to collect aggregate, anonymized information about how visitors interact with the Site (pages visited, session duration, referral sources). This data is used solely for internal service improvement and is not sold or shared with advertising networks.
Email Tracking

Our newsletter emails may contain web beacons (small transparent images) that allow us to determine whether an email was opened and which links were clicked. This information is used to assess newsletter performance and improve content. If you prefer not to be tracked in this way, you may disable image loading in your email client.

Managing Cookies

You may adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site, including your ability to log in to your subscriber account. Instructions for managing cookies are available in your browser's help documentation.

By continuing to use the Site, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this Section. This Cookie Policy forms part of and is incorporated into this Privacy Policy, as referenced in our Terms of Use.

06 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to provide your subscription, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specifically:

When your personal information is no longer required for these purposes, we will delete or anonymize it in accordance with our internal data retention procedures and applicable law.

09 California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act ("CPRA"), including:

  • Right to Know. The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you.
  • Right to Delete. The right to request deletion of personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct. The right to request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.
  • Right to Opt Out of Sale or Sharing. The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information. We do not sell personal information as defined under the CCPA, and we do not share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information. To the extent we collect sensitive personal information (as defined by the CPRA), you have the right to limit its use to purposes authorized by law.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination. The right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us at privacy@nexussigintbrief.com or by mail at the address in Section 14. We will respond to verifiable consumer requests within the timeframes required by the CCPA. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. You may also designate an authorized agent to submit requests on your behalf; authorized agents must provide written authorization from you and verification of their own identity.

We do not sell personal information as defined under the CCPA. We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information for purposes beyond those permitted by the CPRA without your consent.
